Job Placement Services

The biggest benefit of coding bootcamps is the number of successful student outcomes. According to a report, 80% of bootcamp graduates successfully found employment using their software development skills.

Job Search Preparation

A big benefit of coding bootcamps is the built-in job search preparation. A lot of camps have a hands-on focus, and by the end of the curriculum students have built several web applications that serve as great initial content for a software engineering portfolio.

Advanced Curriculum

Coding schools boast their up-to-date curriculum as an advantage over higher education options. University CS degrees are accredited by national and state departments of education, which means their curriculum has to be designed months or years in advance.
